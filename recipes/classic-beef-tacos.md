# Classic Beef Tacos

![Classic Beef Tacos](https://unsplash.com/photos/YIk_xKvKFcs/download?w=800)

## Description

These Classic Beef Tacos are a crowd-pleasing Mexican-inspired dish that's quick, easy, and endlessly customizable. Seasoned ground beef is piled into warm tortillas and topped with fresh veggies, cheese, and your favorite sauces.

## Ingredients

- 500g ground beef
- 8 small flour or corn tortillas
- 1 packet taco seasoning (or 1 tsp each: cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, paprika)
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 avocado, sliced
- Sour cream and salsa for serving
- Salt to taste

## Steps

1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and cook, breaking it apart, until browned.
2. Drain excess fat from the skillet.
3. Add taco seasoning and 1/4 cup of water to the beef. Stir and simmer for 3–5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
4. Warm the tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
5. Assemble the tacos: place beef on each tortilla, then top with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and avocado.
6. Serve with sour cream and salsa on the side.
