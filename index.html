Classic Beef Tacos

These Classic Beef Tacos are a crowd-pleasing Mexican-inspired dish that's quick, easy, and endlessly customizable. Seasoned ground beef is piled into warm tortillas and topped with fresh veggies, cheese, and your favorite sauces.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 8 small flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or 1 tsp each: cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, paprika)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and cook, breaking it apart, until browned.
  2. Drain excess fat from the skillet.
  3. Add taco seasoning and 1/4 cup of water to the beef. Stir and simmer for 3–5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  5. Assemble the tacos: place beef on each tortilla, then top with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and avocado.
  6. Serve with sour cream and salsa on the side.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish that comes together in under 30 minutes. It features a rich, creamy sauce made from eggs, Parmesan cheese, and crispy pancetta — no cream needed. Simple ingredients, big flavor.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Parmesan. Set aside.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook pancetta until crispy. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  4. Remove the skillet from heat. Add the drained spaghetti and toss to coat with the pancetta fat.
  5. Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing quickly. Add pasta water a little at a time until the sauce is creamy.
  6. Season with salt and plenty of black pepper. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Honey Garlic Salmon

Honey Garlic Salmon is a simple yet impressive dish that's packed with flavor. The salmon fillets are pan-seared until golden and glazed with a sweet and savory honey garlic sauce. Perfect for a healthy weeknight dinner in just 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 150g each)
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon slices and parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Pat salmon fillets dry with paper towels. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Mix together honey, minced garlic, soy sauce, and lemon juice in a small bowl. Set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Place salmon fillets skin-side down and cook for 4–5 minutes.
  4. Flip the fillets and pour the honey garlic sauce over them. Cook for another 3–4 minutes, spooning the sauce over the salmon as it cooks.
  5. Remove from heat once the salmon is cooked through and the sauce has thickened slightly.
  6. Garnish with lemon slices and fresh parsley. Serve immediately.
